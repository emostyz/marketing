PRODUCT OVERVIEW
================

AEDRIN is an AI-powered marketing deck platform that transforms business data into professional presentations. The platform uses advanced AI to analyze data, generate insights, and create compelling slides automatically.

CORE GOALS
----------
1. Automate presentation creation from business data
2. Provide AI-powered insights and analysis
3. Enable real-time collaboration on presentations
4. Offer professional templates and design
5. Support multiple data formats and sources
6. Provide enterprise-grade security and scalability

PRIMARY USE CASES
-----------------
1. Business executives creating board presentations
2. Marketing teams generating campaign reports
3. Sales teams creating client presentations
4. Analysts sharing data insights
5. Consultants delivering strategic recommendations
6. Teams collaborating on presentation creation
